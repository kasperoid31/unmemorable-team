## About

**Memory Leak** is a psychological horror mod for Minecraft.

No jump scares. No bosses. No health bars to whittle down.

Just the slow decay of reality — strange sounds, forgotten presences, and the
creeping feeling that you are not the first one here. And not the last.

The mod is designed to be experienced, not explained. The less you know going
in, the better it works. Play it alone. Play it at night.

## Features

- A quiet, atmospheric horror experience built on dread rather than gore
- Reactive world that changes the longer you play
- Subtle audio design — headphones strongly recommended
- No HUD clutter, no objectives, no hand-holding
- Singleplayer-focused; works on servers too

## Installation

1. Install the [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) for Minecraft 26.1.
2. Download [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) and place it in your `mods` folder.
3. Download **Memory Leak** from the [official Modrinth page](https://modrinth.com/project/memoryleak).
4. Drop the `.jar` into your `mods` folder.
5. Launch the game, and turn the lights off.

> **Tip:** Use headphones and play with in-game sound on. A lot of the experience lives in what you hear.
